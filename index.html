Welcome to Saiyan Invaders!

Instructions

  • Frieza has managed to clone himself!

  • Protect the earth from Frieza and his clones by elimating them before they reaching the earth!

  • Move Goku using the left and right keys

  • Shoot kamehamehas using the space bar

  • Watch out for enemy ki blasts! If you are hit, you will lose a life. Take cover behind the shields or DODGE!

  • If the enemies reach the earth, or you run out of lives, you lose!

  • Eliminate all of Frieza's forces to win the game!
